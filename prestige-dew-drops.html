Overview

Prestige Dew Drops is an intimate gated enclave of just 25 ultra-luxury villas set across 5.4 verdant acres along Doddaballapura Main Road in serene North Bangalore. Crafted by the Prestige Group, the community arranges its 3 and 4 BHK residences in five elegant rows, with sweeping 3,508 to 5,868 sq ft layouts that prioritise light, privacy and feng-shui-aligned planning. An expansive 75% of the land is dedicated to open green space, complemented by over thirty curated amenities including a clubhouse, mini theatre and dedicated zones for children and seniors. Positioned away from the city's bustle yet within convenient reach of the airport and Yelahanka, the enclave offers a tranquil, low-density lifestyle increasingly rare near Bengaluru.
